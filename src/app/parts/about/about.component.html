About Me


Hello! I'm Benjamin (Ben for short), a software engineer based in Seattle, WA who has a love for space and cutting-edge technologies. My desire is to advance my skills and experience to take on the impossible and make a difference in the world through the innovation and development of next generation technology. I am innovative, forward thinking, and driven to find and apply both creative and elegant solutions to complex problems using collaboration, big picture thinking, and agile methods. In addition, my time at PopOver and the University of Puget Sound allowed me to demonstrate my ability to design, develop, and maintain software, quickly learn new skills, and successfully and ethically work with and lead a team.

This experience has allowed me to hone my problem-solving skills and gain a great understanding of full-stack development and the Systems Engineering product lifestyle. Since the COVID-19 pandemic I have been keeping my software developing skills honed while I find my next adventure.
